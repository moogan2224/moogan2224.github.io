Week 2 | Jan 22-28

Following the revival of Aggie Bonfire as Student Bonfire, the Neeley Bonfire Crew (Neeley for short) remains fairly active. I reached out to Neeley's 2024 leadership (Backbreaker, Shrek, Kratos, Sub Zero, and Tomahawk) looking to increase their crew recruitment and retention rates. Over the winter break, I gave myself a headstart recreating the official logo with variations, choosing typography and colors, business cards, and deciding the themes
