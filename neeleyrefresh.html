Week 7 & 8 | Feb 26 - Mar 8

I did a lot of research into collapse by reading through the fire commissioner's report and external investigation. It gave me a good reminder to address how saftey is of utmost importance to bonfire and concerns any future parents or participants may

I interviewed Luke Cheatham a former Walton '99 and Greypot. Walton is a formerly all-male dorm and currently all-male crew that focuses on Load. Load is where people manually hand-load and unload logs onto a truck using their shoulders. People stagger themselves along the log by height - tallest to shortest - picking up the log with their bare hands and placing it on their shoulders, lodging it between their head, neck, and shoulders.
